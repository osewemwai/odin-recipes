SukumaWiki

Description

This is a delicacy commonly found within most east african communities

Ingredients

  • Sukuma Wiki
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. wash the leaves of sukumawiki
  2. peel and slice the onion and chop the tomatoes
  3. Heat the cooking pan
  4. Fry the onion until golden brown
  5. Add tomatoes until soft
  6. Once the tomatoes are cooked through add sukumawiki and cover for 5 minutes
  7. Season with salt
  8. Remove from heat and serve with ugali
